# Why Rotor?

## The Privacy Problem in Blockchain

Blockchain technology has revolutionized how we think about trust, transparency, and decentralized systems. However, this transparency comes with a significant drawback: **complete lack of financial privacy**.

Every transaction on public blockchains like Stellar is visible to anyone:
- Transaction amounts
- Sender addresses
- Recipient addresses
- Transaction timing and patterns
- Account balances

This creates several real-world problems:

### For Individuals

- **Financial Surveillance**: Anyone can track your entire transaction history and net worth
- **Personal Safety**: Publicly visible wealth can make you a target
- **Discrimination**: Financial institutions or services may treat you differently based on your transaction history
- **Loss of Autonomy**: Your spending habits, income, and financial relationships are exposed

### For Businesses

- **Competitive Disadvantage**: Competitors can analyze your payments, suppliers, and customer base
- **Price Discrimination**: Vendors may adjust pricing based on visible financial status
- **Trade Secrets**: Business relationships and deal structures become public knowledge
- **Employee Privacy**: Payroll and compensation become traceable

### For the Ecosystem

- **Reduced Adoption**: Privacy concerns prevent mainstream users from adopting blockchain payments
- **Regulatory Challenges**: Lack of confidential transactions limits enterprise adoption
- **Fungibility Issues**: Coins with certain transaction histories may be valued differently

## Why Stellar Needs Privacy

Stellar is designed for fast, low-cost payments and has excellent adoption for cross-border transactions and asset tokenization. However, like most blockchains, it lacks native privacy features. This limits its potential for:

- Private remittances
- Confidential business payments
- Salary distributions
- Personal financial management
- Anonymous donations

## The Rotor Solution

Rotor brings **programmable privacy** to Stellar without compromising security or decentralization:

### Non-Custodial
You maintain full control of your assets throughout the process. Rotor cannot access or freeze your funds.

### Zero-Knowledge Privacy
Using cutting-edge cryptography, Rotor proves transaction validity without revealing sensitive information. The blockchain verifies correctness without seeing the details.

### Regulatory Friendly
Unlike mixing services, Rotor enables legitimate privacy use cases with cryptographic guarantees and optional compliance features.

### Stellar-Native
Built on Soroban smart contracts, Rotor leverages Stellar's speed, low fees, and existing ecosystem while adding privacy as a layer.

### Open and Transparent
The protocol is fully open-source, auditable, and community-governed. Privacy through mathematics, not obscurity.
